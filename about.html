About Medical Arts Pharmacy

A neighborhood pharmacy built on trust, service, and genuine care for our San Leandro community.

Our Story

Pharmacy the way it should be

Medical Arts Pharmacy is an independent, locally owned pharmacy located on E 14th Street in San Leandro, serving families throughout Alameda County. Our motto — "At Your Care" — isn't just a slogan. It's how we approach every prescription we fill and every person who walks through our door.

At a big-chain pharmacy, you're a number in a queue. Here, you're a neighbor. Our pharmacists know their patients by name, follow up on questions, and take the time to make sure every medication is right for you.
