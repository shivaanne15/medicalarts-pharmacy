Our Services

Everything you'd expect from a full-service pharmacy — delivered with the personal touch only an independent pharmacy can offer.

Prescription Filling

Fast, accurate dispensing of new prescriptions from any doctor or hospital, with a careful safety review of every order before it leaves our counter.

Easy Refills

Refilling takes one quick phone call. Have your prescription number handy, and we'll tell you exactly when it will be ready for pickup.

Prescription Transfers

Moving from another pharmacy? Just call us with your current pharmacy's name and your medication list — we handle the entire transfer for you.

Medication Counseling

One-on-one time with a pharmacist to review how your medications work, what to expect, and how to avoid interactions and side effects.

Insurance Coordination

We bill most major insurance plans, Medicare Part D, and Medi-Cal, and we'll work to find you the lowest available price on every fill.

Over-the-Counter Products

Vitamins, supplements, first aid, and everyday health essentials — plus a pharmacist nearby to help you pick the right one.

Medication Synchronization

Taking several medications? We can align your refills to a single monthly pickup, so you make one trip instead of five.

Medication Reviews

A periodic check of everything you take — prescriptions, OTC, and supplements — to catch duplications and interactions early.

Ask a Pharmacist

Not sure about a dose, a symptom, or a product? Call us — real advice from a pharmacist who knows you, at no charge.
